## Description (max 250 words)

**ScrapeFlow** is a Chrome extension that turns web scraping into a point-and-click experience. No scripts. No selectors. No setup.

Here's how it works: open any webpage, click the ScrapeFlow icon, and hover over the data you want. Our **AI auto-detection** instantly identifies tables, lists, and card layouts — just click to capture. Export everything to CSV or JSON with one click.

**Why ScrapeFlow?**

🔍 **AI Auto-Detection** — ScrapeFlow's AI scans the page and highlights extractable data structures automatically. Tables, product grids, directory listings — it just finds them.

📊 **Smart Export** — One-click export to CSV or JSON. Clean, structured data ready for spreadsheets, databases, or analysis tools. Custom filenames included.

⚡ **Zero Code** — No Python. No BeautifulSoup. No Puppeteer. If you can click a mouse, you can scrape data.

**Who's it for?**

- Researchers collecting data from multiple sources
- Marketers gathering competitive intelligence
- Developers who need quick data extraction without writing scripts
- Anyone who's ever copy-pasted from a webpage into a spreadsheet

**Pricing:** Free (50 rows/mo) · Pro $9/mo (unlimited + AI) · Team $19/mo (scheduled scraping + team dashboard)

Works on virtually any website — e-commerce, directories, job boards, real estate portals, news sites. If you can see it in your browser, ScrapeFlow can extract it.

## Maker's Story

I built ScrapeFlow because I was tired of writing brittle scraping scripts that broke every time a website changed its layout. As a developer, I had the skills to scrape — but it still felt like a waste of time for simple data extraction tasks. I wanted something as easy as highlighting text and copying it, but for structured data. So I built it.
