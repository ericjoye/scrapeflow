# Privacy Policy — ScrapeFlow

**Last updated:** June 20, 2026

## Overview

ScrapeFlow is a Chrome extension that helps users extract data from webpages. We are committed to your privacy.

## Data Collection

**ScrapeFlow does NOT collect any user data.**

- All scraping happens locally in your browser
- No data is transmitted to any server
- No analytics or tracking
- No accounts or sign-ups required

## Local Storage

ScrapeFlow stores data locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`:
- Scraped data (until you export or clear it)
- User preferences (theme, export format)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

## Permissions

- **activeTab**: To access the current page for scraping
- **storage**: To save your data locally
- **scripting**: To run scraping logic on the page

## Third-Party Services

ScrapeFlow does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking platforms.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the extension update notes.
